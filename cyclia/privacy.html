PRIVACY POLICY

Cyclia

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Cyclia mobile application ("App") handles user information.

1. Data Controller

The controller of the App is:

  • Name: Lukáš Kubaliak
  • Country: Slovak Republic
  • Email: lukas.bakuliak@gmail.com

The App is operated by a private individual (not a registered business entity).

2. Core Privacy Statement

Cyclia is designed with a strong focus on privacy.

The App:

  • Does not collect any personal data
  • Does not collect any health data
  • Does not require registration or user accounts
  • Does not transmit any user data to the developer
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not use analytics tools or third-party tracking
  • Works fully offline

3. Local Data Storage

All information entered into the App (such as menstrual cycle data) is:

  • Stored exclusively on the user's device
  • Processed locally on the user's device
  • Not accessible to the developer

The developer has no technical ability to access, retrieve, or monitor data stored within the user's device.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

The App is available through a monthly subscription.

Payments are processed exclusively by Apple Inc. via the Apple App Store.

The developer:

  • Does not receive or store payment card details
  • Does not have access to billing information
  • Does not process financial data

Payment processing and related data handling are governed by Apple's own Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

The App:

  • Does not use Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar tools
  • Does not integrate advertising networks
  • Does not share data with third parties

6. Internet Connectivity

Cyclia functions offline and does not require an internet connection for its core functionality.

7. GDPR Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), individuals normally have rights such as access, rectification, erasure, restriction, and data portability.

However, since Cyclia does not collect or process personal data, these rights are generally not applicable in practice.

If a user contacts the developer via email, the email address provided will be processed solely for the purpose of responding to the inquiry.

8. Data Security

Because the App does not collect or transmit personal data, the risk of data breaches is significantly minimized.

The security of locally stored data depends on the security measures of the user's device (such as passcode protection, Face ID, or Touch ID).

9. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 years and older.

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The developer reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time.

The updated version will be made available within the App, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.

11. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact:

📩 lukas.bakuliak@gmail.com

Final Statement

Cyclia is built with privacy as a core principle. No personal or health data is collected, processed, or transmitted by the App.
